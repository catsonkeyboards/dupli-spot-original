Privacy Policy for Dupli-Spot Web Application

Last Updated: [13 August 2023]

Welcome to Dupli-Spot, a web application designed to help you detect and remove duplicate tracks between different Spotify playlists. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Data Collection

When you use Dupli-Spot, we collect the following information:

  • Spotify account details necessary for authentication.
  • Spotify Library, Playlist, Like Songs, Collaborative Playist and Private and Public Playlist information to detect and remove duplicate tracks.

Data Usage

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Authenticate users with their Spotify account.
  • Analyze public, private and collaborative playlists to detect duplicate tracks.
  • Provide the option to remove duplicate tracks from selected playlists.

Data Storage

Your data is stored temporarily while you're using the application and is deleted once your session ends. We do not store your data on our servers for extended periods.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All processes are carried out within the application, and no external entities have access to your data.

User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect and how we use it.
  • Request deletion of any data we might have stored during your session.
  • Opt-out of any future data collection.
  • Disconnect your Spotify account from Dupli-Spot at any time. Instructions on how to do so can be found in the 'Settings' section of Spotify.

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to ensure the safety of your data. While no online service is entirely secure, we strive to protect your information to the best of our abilities and in line with industry standards.

Compliance with Spotify's Developer Policy

We regularly review and update our Privacy Policy to ensure ongoing compliance with Spotify's Developer Policy. We ensure that our functionalities do not fall under Spotify's list of prohibited applications and that any monetization strategies align with Spotify's commercial use restrictions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review our Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@soundniccolo.com.

Technology Used:

Spotify Web API, Marked.js, HTML, Javascript, CSS, Vercel, GitHub, Morsan font by Typebae, Gazpacho font by Santi Rey

List of Spotify API scopes: playlist-modify-public playlist-modify-private playlist-read-private playlist-read-collaborative

This web app uses the Spotify Web API to manage user's playlists and saved tracks.

Dupli-Spot will only remove user-selected tracks, leaving other playlists and saved songs untouched.
